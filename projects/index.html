The CVE² computes the power series coefficients of the classical virial expansion of the grand-canonical partition function. Its creation is the subject of my undergraduate honors thesis. Since my defense, I have continued to work with my advisor, Dr. Joaquín Drut, on extending its capabilities further. We recently released a preprint covering our latest developments.
