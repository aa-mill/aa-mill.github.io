< About />

I'm a first-year (G1) graduate student at Harvard University pursuing a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics. I work in the Computational Science and Engineering Lab under the advisement of Dr. Petros Koumoutsakos. I graduated in May 2023 from The University of North Carolinia at Chapel Hill with majors in Applied Mathematics and Astrophysics and a minor in Statistics and Analytics. At UNC, I was advised by Dr. Joaquín Drut, with whom I developed the Classical Virial Expansion Engine (CVE²).

I created this website so that I can share papers, code, and blog posts from a central location. I wrote the Home, About, CV, and Projects pages using HTML, (S)CSS, and Bootstrap, and I used the Hexo static site generator to take care of the functionality needed for the Blog page. Aside from providing me with a nice organizational tool, I hope that this website can serve as an invitation to get in touch if there's anything you'd like to talk with me about. I'm always happy to learn and meet new people, and I'd love to hear your story.

- Aaron
