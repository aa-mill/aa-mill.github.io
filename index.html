New Paper Coming
Calculating and Resumming the Classical Virial Expansion Using Automated Algebra

Dr. Joaquín Drut and I have uploaded a preprint of our most recent work involving the Classical Virial Expansion Engine (CVE²). In it, we use schematic model potentials to exactly calculate the virial coefficients of a classical gas up to 6th order, and we use them (along with Padé-Borel resummation techniques) to investigate the convergence properties of the virial expansion of basic thermodynamic quantities.
